Market overview

Take a brief overview of the most recent market movements spanning different sectors. The foreign exchange (forex) market serves as a platform where individuals can engage in buying, selling, hedging, and speculating on currency pair exchange rates. Recognized as the most fluid market globally, the forex market exhibits high liquidity due to the cash nature of its assets. With a staggering daily transaction volume surpassing $5 trillion, it outperforms both the futures and equity markets combined. Similar to over-the-counter (OTC) markets, the forex market operates in a decentralized manner, incorporating a worldwide network of computers and brokers.
